  • About Us

Hello! Im Ryan Fu, Regional Vice President at RossCo Real Estate Group in Wisconsin. I began my serial Entrepreneurship in 1993 and I eventually found a career where there was consistency, good pay, and stability. Who needs that, so I quickly gave it up in 1999 for a commission-only real estate sales career where I had no experience or knowledge at the time. Sheer grit or maybe stubbornness got me through all the tough times as I created systems and processes for my deficiencies. Yes, I have lots and lots of systems and processes! After creating a consistency once again I was on for a search for inconsistencies. I have a passion and obligation to help others not go through the pains that I experienced by coaching and helping them find their solutions. I continue to coach and train daily and feel its how I am wired and my obligation to not only create great relationships and share my knowledge but to constantly learn, i.e. fail forward every day. I and always searching to surround myself with people who strive to make themselves better. My motto has always been "Leave Things Better Than You Found Them."

If you share any of these characteristics and want to follow or be part of my evolving tribe, please reach out to me and I would love to hear your story. If you want to get your Wisconsin Real Estate License find out more here: Get Your Real Estate License
